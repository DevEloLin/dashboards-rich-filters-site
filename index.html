The Product

Six dashboards. One unified view.

Each module is purposefully designed to answer a specific question your stakeholders ask — without leaving the Jira dashboard.

— 01 / Health

Project Health Dashboard

A single source of truth for the question every PM dreads: "Where do we actually stand?"

Aggregates completion rates, overdue items, blocked tickets, and unassigned work into a consolidated scorecard. Color-coded indicators surface what needs attention before the next stand-up — eliminating the manual JQL queries that drain twenty minutes from every status meeting.

Completion % Overdue tracking Blocker detection Workload signals
— 02 / Portfolio

Portfolio Overview for PMOs

When you steward ten projects, scrolling through ten Jira boards isn't a strategy — it's a tax.

Portfolio Overview rolls up multi-project status, resource allocation, and delivery pace into one consolidated panel. Drill from the portfolio view straight into the underlying issues with a single click — keeping leadership conversations focused on outcomes rather than ticket archaeology.

Multi-project rollup Click-through to issues PMO-ready
— 03 / Risk

Risk Analysis · Ishikawa Diagram

A boardroom-grade fishbone for the risks hiding in your backlog.

Categorizes signals across People, Process, Quality, and Schedule — automatically derived from issue patterns such as bug fix rate, blocked items, and sprint slippage. The result is a visual conversation starter your engineering leads can take into a steering committee without rebuilding the slide.

Note: Risk analysis is provided as a reference tool and should be validated by project teams.

4M categorization Auto-aggregated signals Boardroom-ready
— 04 / Milestones

Release Timeline & Milestones

Every release deserves a clear story arc — not a spreadsheet.

Visualizes upcoming, overdue, and released milestones along a chronological timeline anchored to today. Counters surface the totals that matter — 20 total · 20 upcoming · 20 overdue · 0 released — paired with a scrollable list of versions ready for grooming or executive review.

Timeline view Overdue surfacing Release-aware
— 05 / Components

Statistics by Components

Workload concentration is the silent killer of velocity. Visualize it.

Decomposes your project into its component teams or modules, surfacing distribution across status, priority, and assignee in a single sortable matrix. Identify which corner of the codebase is carrying the load — and which is being neglected — long before retrospectives reveal it the hard way.

Component breakdown Workload signals Sortable matrix
— 06 / Effort

Time Spent by Assignee

Where the hours actually went — visualized in a single elegant chart.

Aggregates Jira worklogs by team member, exposing effort distribution across the people doing the work. Catch silent overload, surface unbilled time, and rebalance the next sprint with data instead of intuition. Filter by time range — last 7 days, 30 days, this quarter, or any custom window.

Worklog aggregation Per-assignee view Custom time ranges
The Engine

Rich filtering, applied everywhere.

A single filter panel powers every chart in the gadget — change one selection and every visualization re-renders in step.

★

Multi-Select Filters

Filter by projects, assignees, priorities, and statuses simultaneously. Selections compile to JQL automatically.

⤳

Cross-Chart Sync

Every chart on the dashboard shares the same filter state. One click updates the whole view.

⏱

Smart Time Ranges

Last 7 days, 30 days, 90 days, this year, or a custom date window — applied across every visualization.

▦

Recent Activity Feed

Stay current with a chronological stream of issue updates, status changes, and team movement.

⏶

Issue Trends
