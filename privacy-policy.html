Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 30, 2026

Summary: Dashboards Rich Filters does NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your Jira data. All data processing happens in real-time within your browser and Atlassian's secure infrastructure.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how elolin ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use Dashboards Rich Filters ("the App"), a dashboard gadget for Atlassian Jira Cloud available on the Atlassian Marketplace.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices. This policy complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and Atlassian's Marketplace Partner Agreement requirements.

2. Data We Access

To provide dashboard functionality, our App requests the following Atlassian scopes:

Permission Purpose Data Accessed
read:jira-work Display issue statistics and charts Issues, projects, boards, sprints
read:jira-user Populate assignee filter dropdown User display names, account IDs
read:sprint:jira-software Sprint-based filtering Sprint names, states, dates
read:project:jira Project filter functionality Project names, keys

3. Data We Do NOT Collect or Store

We want to be absolutely clear about what we do NOT do:

  • No Data Storage: We do not store any Jira data on our servers
  • No External Transmission: Your data never leaves Atlassian's infrastructure
  • No Analytics on Your Data: We do not analyze or process your Jira content
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We never share your data with third parties
  • No Cookies for Tracking: We do not use tracking cookies
  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect personal information

How It Works: All data is fetched in real-time via Atlassian's Forge platform, processed in your browser, and displayed on your dashboard. When you close the dashboard, the data is gone.

4. Data Processing Location

Our App runs entirely on Atlassian Forge, which means:

  • All code executes within Atlassian's secure cloud infrastructure
  • Data processing complies with Atlassian's security standards
  • No data is transferred to external servers
  • Processing occurs in the same region as your Atlassian instance

5. Data Retention

Since we do not store any data, there is no data retention period. Data is:

  • Fetched when you load the dashboard
  • Displayed in your browser temporarily
  • Discarded when you navigate away or close the browser

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Since we don't store data, there is no data to access
  • Right to Rectification: Not applicable as we don't store data
  • Right to Erasure: Not applicable as we don't store data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can uninstall the App at any time
  • Right to Data Portability: Not applicable as we don't store data
  • Right to Object: You can uninstall the App at any time

7. Your Rights Under CCPA

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected (we collect none)
  • Request deletion of personal information (not applicable)
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

8. Children's Privacy

Our App is designed for business use and is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Security

Security measures in place:

  • App runs on Atlassian Forge with SOC 2 Type II compliance
  • All communications use HTTPS encryption
  • No external API calls or data transmission
  • Regular security reviews as part of Atlassian Marketplace requirements

10. Third-Party Services

Our App does not integrate with any third-party services outside of Atlassian's platform. The only external service involved is the Atlassian Jira Cloud API, which is governed by Atlassian's own privacy policy.

11. Changes to This Policy

  • Communicated via Atlassian Marketplace update notes for significant changes
